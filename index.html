About CK Prime Paint

When you call CK Prime Paint, you get Chris. He'll come out to quote it, he'll prep it, and he'll paint it himself. Just a local tradesperson who takes real pride in the work.

Chris has been painting homes across the Ryde district and North Shore for over 10 years. Dozens of jobs – interior, exterior, roof to fence – and every single one finished to the same standard. Customers come back, and they refer their friends.
